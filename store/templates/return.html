Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Please read our return policy carefully.

1. Return Period

You may return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. The item must be in its original condition, unused, and in the original packaging.

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Opened or used electrical components
  • Custom or special order items
  • Software and digital products
  • Items damaged due to misuse or improper installation

3. Return Process

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team at returns@tjsystems.co.za with your order number and reason for return. We will provide you with a return authorization and instructions.

4. Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days. The refund will be issued to your original payment method. Shipping costs are non-refundable.

5. Exchanges

If you need to exchange an item for a different product, please contact us. You will be responsible for any price difference and additional shipping costs.

6. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement or refund at no additional cost to you.
