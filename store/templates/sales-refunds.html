Sales and Refunds Policy

Sales Policy

All sales are final unless otherwise stated. Prices are subject to change without notice. We reserve the right to limit quantities and refuse orders.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds are available for items returned within 30 days of delivery, provided they are in original condition and packaging. Some items may be excluded from returns.

Refund Process

To request a refund, contact our customer service team. Once your return is received and inspected, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for refunds:

  • Opened or used electrical components
  • Custom or special order items
  • Items damaged due to customer misuse
  • Digital products and software

Shipping Costs

Original shipping costs are non-refundable. For returns due to our error, we will cover return shipping costs.

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund within 10 business days, please contact your bank or credit card company first. If the issue persists, contact us at refunds@tjsystems.co.za.
